What Changed

Root cause: extract_chords_from_track() cleared its note buffer on every new note_on event. Arpeggiated music never accumulated ‚â•2 simultaneous notes, so zero chords were emitted.

Fix: Time-window grouping algorithm that collects all notes within 1 beat. Also changed track selection from "highest simultaneous polyphony" to "most total note-on events."

Files: app/services/midi_parser.py, main.py

Risk Areas

  • Chord extraction for block-chord songs like Corcovado (regression from algorithm rewrite)
  • Track selection change could pick different track for multi-track MIDI files
  • Time-window (1 beat) may group notes too aggressively for fast passages
